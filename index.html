Why I Care:

As someone who's experienced financial struggles firsthand, I understand the importance of timely and fair compensation. Service workers deserve better. With Oihva, we're reclaiming the original purpose of tipping: recognizing exceptional service and rewarding hardworking individuals.

About

As a passionate advocate for fair compensation and transparency, I've dedicated myself to revolutionizing the tipping industry with Oihva, a Lightning Network-powered wallet app.My mission is to empower workers to reclaim control over their hard-earned tips, preventing employers from co-opting or manipulating them. I believe that customers deserve to know their generosity is reaching the intended recipient, and that workers deserve to keep their rightful earnings.

Through Oihva, I aim to bridge the gap between intention and reality in the tipping process. By utilizing blockchain technology and direct, peer-to-peer transactions, Oihva ensures that tips go directly to the worker, eliminating intermediaries and potential exploitation. My goal is to create a more equitable and transparent tipping ecosystem, where customers can trust that their appreciation is received by those who truly deserve it. By restoring control to workers and promoting fairness, Oihva seeks to transform the service industry and foster a culture of genuine gratitude.
